Executive Profile

Dr. Benjamin Abongo is a distinguished leader in the Kenyan insurance industry, currently serving as the Chief Operations Officer at Capex Life Assurance.

With a career spanning over two decades, Dr. Abongo has become a cornerstone of the life insurance sector in East Africa. His leadership is defined by a strategic balance between Underwriting precision and Operational innovation.

A scholar-practitioner at heart, he holds a PhD in Business Administration from KeMU. His academic rigour informs his professional approach, ensuring data-driven decision-making across all portfolios.

His global perspective is shaped by advanced training in India and South Africa, bringing international best practices to the local competitive market dynamics.
