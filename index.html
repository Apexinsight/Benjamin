Executive Profile

Dr. Benjamin Abongo is a distinguished leader in the Kenyan insurance industry with over 20 years of hands-on experience. As the Chief Operations Officer at Capex Life Assurance, he oversees the strategic balance of Life Business operations, including Underwriting, Claims management, and Reinsurance portfolios.

A scholar-practitioner, Dr. Abongo holds a PhD in Business Administration from Kenya Methodist University. His expertise is globally informed, having undergone specialized professional training in Kenya, India, and South Africa, focusing on the intersection of internal marketing and competitive market dynamics.
